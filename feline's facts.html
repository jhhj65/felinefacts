Feline’s Facts

Domestic Shorthair

Most cat breed around the world, comes in a variety of colours, is a mix of several breeds but is labelled as one due to confusion, very social and affectionate pets, this breed is very playful and prefer exploring but also loves time with their owner.

Ragdoll Cat

Beautiful longhair cats, their coats come in a variety of colours such as white, chocolate, blue and red, friendly breed with behaviour akin to dogs, unlike most cats these ragdolls enjoy water, their coats are high maintenance and need frequent brushing to avoid knots and matting.

Persian Cat

Elegant longhair cats, peaceful and quiet cats that prefer to relax not as active as other breeds, come in a variety of colours, high maintenance due their gorgeous coat which requires regular grooming.

Now that you know about some facts of very popular cat breeds, here are ways we can support our furry friends. Cat welfare is important yet many face struggles such as neglect, homelessness, and health issues. By encouraging responsible pet ownership practises such as spaying and neutering to control cat population and proving regular vet care. We can improve the lives of cat by encouraging adopting and not seeking unethical breeders, regular immunization, proper nutrition’s, and mental stimulation.
